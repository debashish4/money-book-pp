Privacy Policy for MoneyBook

Effective Date: 19th April 2025

At MoneyBook, your privacy is our priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how your data is handled when using the app.

1. Overview

MoneyBook is a personal finance manager designed to work offline. Your data is stored on your device and is not collected or transmitted by us unless you enable optional backup features.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (e.g., name, email)
  • Device information
  • Location data
  • Analytics or usage data

3. Data Stored Locally

All your finance records, categories, settings, and preferences are stored on your device only. No external servers are involved.

4. Optional Backup to Google Drive

If you choose to use the optional backup feature:

  • The app will request access to your Google Drive via secure Google authentication.
  • Only files created by MoneyBook are read/written.
  • No other Drive files are accessed.

5. Data Security

While we don't store any data ourselves, we ensure the app only requests essential permissions. You are in control of your backups and data.
